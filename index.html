Eligibility

Candidates must be in possesion of a recognized university degree and a current temporary/practicing licence issued by the registration council.

Requirements

Candidates are required to attach all necessary credentials to their platform they include:

  • Application letter(with phone number) addressed to The chief Medical director, Rivers State University Teachiing Hospital, 6-8 Harley Street, Old GRA, Port harcourt, Rivers State.
  • Curriculum Vitae.
  • Passport photograph (2 copies).
  • Licence/registration.
  • Degree certificate or Notification(statement) of results.
  • Waec/O'level certificate.
  • First school leaving certificate.
  • L.G.A identification letter.
  • Birth certificate/statutory declaration of age.
  • letters from two(2) senior citizens.
